Concept
This is a one or two player tank battle game. The two tanks must battle to fight each other with a limited number of shots and health. Terrain is used for tacitcal advantage

Rules
-The two tanks are the same
-you can go anywhere which is surrounded by mountains
-20 shots
-100 health
-Each shot does 10 - 100 damage depending on area hit.
-last one standing wins

Requirements
-Two tanks, controlled by either AI or player
-Spare parts revive health
-Ammo boxes refil ammo
-SFX: Gun firing, reloading, explosion, barrel moving, turret moving, engine sound, repair sound
-Static mesh: Simple tank, tracks, body, turret, barrel
-Textures: Later add for visual flare
-Music: Background music
